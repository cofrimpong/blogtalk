# Compound Learning for Builders

Most people optimize for intensity. The highest performers optimize for **compounding**.

Compounding learning means each project increases the quality of your next project by default.

## Build your loop

1. **Capture**
   - Keep short decision notes: what you expected, what happened, why.
2. **Compress**
   - Convert experience into reusable principles.
3. **Codify**
   - Turn principles into templates, checklists, and defaults.
4. **Compound**
   - Reuse your own systems so quality rises while cognitive load falls.

## The practical advantage

This approach does two things simultaneously:

- improves your speed,
- and raises your strategic ceiling.

Talent starts the race. Systems decide who keeps accelerating.
